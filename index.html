I Safia Naureen Malik, working as Scientific Officer (Soil Science) at Soil and Water Conservation Research Institute Chakwal for the last ten years. I am well equipped with modern techniques in the field of Soil Science, particularly in the field of land and water management, soil fertility/soil health, rainfed agriculture, water saving techniques, and efficient use of stored rainwater. I have the ability to work in all situations either in the field, lab or in official matters/correspondence. I am also involved in capacity building of farmers, professionals, and service providers in the field of land and water management including rainwater harvesting techniques, high-efficiency irrigation systems (HEIS: drip, bubbler), soil conservation from soil erosion through rainwater, on-farm preparation of compost and its use for improvement in soil health.

Projects.

1.Watershed Rehabilitation and Irrigation Improvement in Pakistan. Demonstration and dissemination of proven technologies to help the farmers of rural Pakistan. (2011-2015 / USDA-ICARDA)

Development of demonstration sites for low-cost water-saving techniques (High-Efficiency irrigation system, gypsum for moisture conservation, micro catchments (square, half-moon shape for flat land and V shape for sloppy lands) Dissemination of project activities to the male and female farmers of the rainfed area especially the Potowar region Capacity Building of female farmers on rooftop rainwater harvesting for kitchen gardening Capacity Building of Professionals from FATA, KPK, and Baluchistan regarding Project activities Prepared Brochures both in national and international language Prepared CDs for a better understanding of the technology Delivered 7 radio talks under this project about the technology.

2. Improving Soil Fertility and Soil Health. (2014-2016 / USDA-ICARDA )

As a focal person of the project responsible for the Development of Demonstration sites for the use of On-farm Composting technology for kitchen gardening and production of off-season vegetables under tunnel farming. Dissemination about the methods of compost making to the female community of rainfed areas. The main objective behind this was to improve the livelihood of female farmers through vegetable production. Trained 1200 female farmers and 200 Professionals about the making of compost at the Household and farm level Prepared Brochures and CDs about the technology in Urdu and English Language Trained 300 females working under the umbrella of Social NGO’s. Work in close interaction with the Social NGO (PODA, NRSP, SARBAN, Log Sanjhn) Delivered 10 radio talks about the technology in Punjabi and English.

3. Strategic Strengthening of Flood Warning and Management Capacity of Pakistan Phase II. (2016-2017 / UNESCO-JICA)

Resource person for the capacity building of farmers affected by flood and drought Trained 200 farmers and Professionals from FATA, KPK, Baluchistan Capacity building of farmers from Rot kohi irrigation system.

Responsible for creating the linkages between farmers, Public and Private sector to increase adoption and diffusion of low-cost proven technologies Working in close coordination with Agriculture Extension, Soil conservation department, and Local NGOs for awareness of the water-saving technologies.
